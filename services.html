NPC Info

MarshTon Baked Goods
- LadyRibbons buys baked goods in a house with a blue awning on MarshTon. These baked good include but are not limited to: Pies, Bread, Bagels, Apple Turnovers, Muffins, and Cookies.
Dockhouse Wood
- SparLad buys logs for $200 and sticks for $30 in the Prairie Dockhouse.
Desert Twine and Feathers
- BadlandsLass buys feathers and twine on Amber Sands. She only buys feathers in bundles of 4.
Angled Quartz
- MadamGoldFire buys quartz on the upper floor of AngleTon Inn
TigerTon Golden Feathers
- MadamOcelot buys Golden Feathers in TigerTon. You must complete her quest before you can sell them to her.
Horse Painter
- MissHolidayCheer does full horse body painting from a selection of holiday colour which change monthly.
